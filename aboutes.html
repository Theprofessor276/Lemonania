Our Totally Real* History

Welcome to Lemonania, the world’s first restaurant dedicated to the fine art of lemon-based cuisine. Our history is as rich and zesty as our menu. Please enjoy this 100% accurate timeline of how Lemonania changed the world, one lemon at a time.

Lemonania Timeline

  • Ancient Times (Before Rome): In the mists of prehistory, a group of visionary cooks discovered the magic of lemons and began a culinary tradition that would survive the rise and fall of empires.
  • 1743: Sir Isaac Lemon squeezes his first lemon and has a vision: “What if every food was improved by more lemon?”
  • 1756: Lemonania opens its first location in the bustling city of Lemonton. Patrons are both confused and delighted.
  • 1801: The Great Lemon Famine forces us to invent “Imaginary Lemon Soup.” Still a bestseller.
  • 1927: Lemonania’s “Lemon Pie à la Lemon” wins the “Most Lemons in a Single Dish” award.
  • 1984: Our mascot, Sir Squeezalot, debuts. Children everywhere are unsure how to feel.
  • 2024: Lemonania launches its website, forever altering the course of citrus history.
  • 2025: After running the restaurant since before the rise of Rome, our previous head chef retires. TheLemonMaster steps up as the new head chef, bringing fresh zest and vision to our timeless culinary legacy.
“When life gave us lemons, we opened a restaurant.”
- Sir Isaac Lemon, Founder

*Some, or all, of this history may be slightly exaggerated.
